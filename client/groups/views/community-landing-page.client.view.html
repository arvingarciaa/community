Main Features

Virtual Spaces / Groups

  • Groups are commodities from PCAARRD.
  • Groups are classified by industry, sector, ISP, and specific commodity
  • Group members can react and comment on posts.
  • Group members can read private posts.

Categorized Posts

  • Posts are categorized by Question, Incident Report, Event, Media, Advertisement, News, and Others.
  • Post's input fields depend on its category.
  • Posts can be viewed by category.
  • Posts analysis in every group is displayed.

Community Feed

  • Community Feed is composed of latest posts from the joined groups.
  • Community Feed allows reacting to posts without going to the groups.
  • Community Feed is always up-to-date.
